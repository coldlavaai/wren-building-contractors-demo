Our Story

Quality Craftsmanship,
Honest Pricing

Wren Building Contractors was founded in 2012 by brothers Callum and Fraser MacLeod — two time-served tradesmen frustrated by the inconsistency and unreliability they saw across the industry. Their mission was simple: build a company that does what it says, when it says, for the price it says.

Starting with just a van and a tool kit, the brothers built their reputation job by job across Southampton's New Town and Leith. Word spread quickly, and today Wren Building Contractors employs 24 full-time tradespeople covering building, plumbing, electrical and joinery — all under one roof. We've completed over 4,000 projects ranging from small repairs to multi-million pound renovations.

Every member of our team is fully qualified, insured and vetted. We hold Gas Safe, NAPIT and Checkatrade accreditations, and every job comes with a written guarantee. We don't ask for deposits, we don't inflate quotes, and we always leave your property spotless.
